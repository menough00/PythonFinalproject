Inventory

Consumer will have inventory control by using MyPantryy.com for their household food items and essentials. Once a user creates account they will be able to update their inventory on the update page. A user then can search for item or create custom items, modify their items with quantity, cost for per item, and expiration for the item. This will then update to the current inventory for the user. On the current inventory the user will be able to see whats in their pantry, refrigerator and essential they have in stock.
